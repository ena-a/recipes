Easy Broccoli

Description

Deliciously seasoned broccoli, fast and nutritious!

This recipe was inspired by a recipe shared with me by my friend Linda A.

Ingredients

Note: Ingredient measurements are approximate and/or to taste.

  • 2 cups Broccoli florets, cooked and drained
  • 1/4 cup Oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional:
    • Pinch of red pepper flakes
    • 1-1/2 teaspoons Soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Broccoli florets may be cooked by method of your preference, that is, microwave, steamed, boiled.
  2. In separate small pan, heat oil, add red pepper flakes. Lightly brown red pepper flakes -- be careful not to burn them. The goal is to get the oil very hot. Remove pan from heat.
  3. Carefully drizzle hot oil over cooked broccoli. Oil will sizzle, and may splatter.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Gently toss, being careful not to break broccoli florets.
